About Test Calgary

Founded in 2018, Test Calgary emerged from a simple observation: thousands of qualified professionals were failing their certification exams not because they lacked knowledge, but because they lacked effective study resources and guidance. Our founder, a certified educator and tech entrepreneur, partnered with industry experts to create a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with proven educational methodologies. What started as a small initiative has grown into a comprehensive learning ecosystem serving over 50,000 certified professionals across Canada.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to democratize access to high-quality certification preparation and empower professionals to achieve their career aspirations. We believe that success in certification exams should not be limited by geography, socioeconomic status, or access to expensive tutoring services. By combining adaptive learning technology, expert instruction, and community support, we've created a platform where every student has the opportunity to excel. Our core values—integrity, excellence, innovation, and student success—guide every decision we make, ensuring that Test Calgary remains the most trusted name in Calgary certification preparation.
